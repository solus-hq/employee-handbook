Startup Culture

The startup culture is baked into who we are as a company. We move quickly—rapidly releasing new features, deploying updates frequently, and utilizing the latest technology. Our work environment is modern, fun, and efficient. We set ambitious goals and strive to disrupt the industries we enter. Our team is tight-knit and comprised of people who are passionate about technology. Everything you’d expect from a startup.

What sets us apart

The difference is, we’ve been around since 2009. We’re highly profitable and growing consistently. We’re not waiting to reach profitability or dependent on external funding to pay the bills. As such, our team is free to focus on producing amazing work. And unlike a typical startup, we’re not expected to work more than 40 hours per week.
